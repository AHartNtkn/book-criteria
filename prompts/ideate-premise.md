## Your Task

Generate 10 story concept ideas. Each should be:
- A distinct direction, not a variation on the same idea
- Specific enough to build a novel from (not just a theme or genre)
- Articulable in 2-3 sentences: who is the protagonist, what do they want, what's in the way, and what makes this concept compelling

For each idea, include:
- **Concept**: 2-3 sentence pitch
- **What makes it interesting**: Why this is worth a novel (not just a short story or a premise)
- **The hard part**: What would be genuinely difficult to execute well — the thing that, if pulled off, would make this remarkable

Push toward the ambitious. Safe, familiar concepts are easy to generate. Reach for concepts that would make a reader say "I've never seen that before" or "I didn't know I wanted that."
