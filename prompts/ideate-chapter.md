## Your Task

Generate 5 distinct ideas for how this chapter could play out. Each must:
- Deliver on the chapter's purpose as defined in the novel plan
- Be consistent with completed chapters (don't contradict what's happened)
- Be a genuinely different approach, not a variation on the same structure

For each idea, include:
- **Approach**: 3-5 sentences describing how this chapter would unfold — what scenes it would contain, what dramatic shape it would take, how it would accomplish its purpose
- **What's distinctive**: Why this approach is different from the obvious version
- **Opportunity**: What this approach makes possible that the obvious version doesn't — a character moment, a structural payoff, a tonal shift, a thematic deepening
- **Risk**: What would be hardest to execute about this approach

Push toward the non-obvious. The first idea that comes to mind is the one every LLM would generate. The third and fourth ideas are where the interesting territory lives.
