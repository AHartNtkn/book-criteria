## Output Format

For each enhancement:

```
## Enhancement: [One-line description]

**The current moment**: [Quote or reference the specific passage/beat]

**The opportunity**: [What could be done — specific enough to act on]

**Why this is worth attempting**: [What the scene gains — and what it might do for the novel beyond this scene]

**Example direction**: [A few sentences showing the tonal or structural direction, NOT a full rewrite — just enough to convey the idea]
```

Generate 3-5 enhancements. Quality over quantity. One brilliant suggestion that makes the author see the scene differently is worth more than five minor tweaks.
