## Your Task

Read all 25 candidate approaches. Then:

### 1. Identify the strongest elements

Look for:
- Scene ideas that create genuine dramatic momentum
- Structural shapes that serve the chapter's purpose in unexpected ways
- Character moments that deepen arcs established in earlier chapters
- Tonal choices that create productive contrast with surrounding chapters
- Information reveals timed for maximum impact
- Relationship dynamics that advance through action rather than conversation

### 2. Evaluate against the novel plan

The chapter must deliver on its stated purpose. But "deliver" doesn't mean "execute literally." Some candidate approaches may find a better way to accomplish the chapter's purpose than the novel plan envisioned. That's desirable — as long as the purpose is served and the connections to adjacent chapters are maintained.

### 3. Synthesize

Produce a chapter concept that draws from the best elements. The concept should describe:
- The dramatic shape of the chapter (how tension moves)
- The key scenes and what each accomplishes
- How it connects to what came before and sets up what comes after
- What makes this version of the chapter ambitious rather than competent

## What Makes This Ambitious
[The non-obvious choice this chapter makes — the thing that elevates it beyond competent execution]
