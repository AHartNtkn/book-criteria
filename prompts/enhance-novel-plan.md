## Your Task

Read the novel plan and brainstorm 3-5 enhancement opportunities. Each should be:

- **High-payoff**: If executed well, it would meaningfully elevate the novel
- **Non-obvious**: Not something a mechanical revision would produce
- **Specific**: Not "make it better" but a concrete structural or conceptual change
- **Ambitious**: The kind of thing a skilled human author might attempt on their best day

### Types of enhancements to consider

**Structural reconfigurations**
- Could reordering chapters create dramatic irony that doesn't currently exist?
- Could a subplot that's currently parallel be woven into the main plot to create mutual pressure?
- Could the midpoint be restructured so the second half of the novel is a fundamentally different kind of story than the first half?

**Deepening through constraint**
- Could restricting the POV for a stretch create productive mystery or tension?
- Could removing a character force others to fill roles they're not prepared for?
- Could withholding a piece of information from the reader (or from a character) transform the meaning of several chapters?

**Thematic amplification**
- Could the antagonist's goal be made more sympathetic, creating genuine moral ambiguity rather than clear opposition?
- Could the protagonist's flaw be made more structurally load-bearing — not just a trait but the engine that drives complications?
- Could a recurring image or motif be threaded through the plan to accumulate meaning?

**Emotional architecture**
- Is there a missed opportunity for catharsis — a payoff that the plan has set up but doesn't fully exploit?
- Could the emotional trajectory of the novel be made more surprising — a unexpected valley where peaks are expected, or vice versa?
- Could a relationship that's currently functional be made more complex — conflicting loyalties, competing needs, irreconcilable values?

**Raising the difficulty**
- What's the hardest version of this story? Not the most complex, but the version that requires the most skill to pull off?
- Where is the plan playing it safe? Where could it take a genuine risk?

## Output Format

For each enhancement:

```
## Enhancement: [One-line description]

**What exists now**: [Brief description of the current state in the plan]

**The opportunity**: [What could be done differently — specific and concrete]

**Why this is worth attempting**: [What the novel gains if this works]

**The risk**: [What could go wrong — why this is hard]

**How to execute**: [Concrete steps — which chapters change, what moves where]
```

Generate 3-5 enhancements. Quality over quantity — a single brilliant suggestion is worth more than five mediocre ones.
